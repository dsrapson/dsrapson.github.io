Curriculum Vitae Google Scholar

I study how consumers and firms respond to incentives and what this means for energy, environmental, and economic policy. My research examines the tensions in the energy transition between effective government intervention and the costs of poorly-designed policies.

Research Interests: Energy & Environmental Economics, Industrial Organization, Applied Microeconomics
